Smart Locks and Doorbells: Features, Safety, and Top Picks

The rise of smart home technology has revolutionized home security, with smart locks and doorbells leading the charge. These devices offer convenience, enhanced security, and remote monitoring, making them essential for modern homeowners.

Understanding Smart Locks and Doorbells

What Are Smart Locks?

Smart locks replace traditional deadbolts with keyless entry systems that can be controlled via a smartphone, voice commands, or even biometric authentication. Key features include:

  • Remote Access: Lock or unlock doors from anywhere using an app
  • Temporary Access Codes: Grant time-limited access to guests or service providers
  • Integration with Smart Home Systems: Works with Alexa, Google Assistant, or Apple HomeKit
  • Auto-Lock/Unlock: Automatically secures your door when you leave or unlocks when you approach
